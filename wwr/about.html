Splash White Water Rafting

At Splash White Water Rafting, we live for adventure, and we love sharing that thrill with others.
For over two decades, we've been guiding thrill-seekers down some of the most exhilarating rivers in the region.

Whether you're a first-timer or a seasoned rafter, our experienced guides ensure a safe, fun,
and unforgettable ride through roaring rapids and stunning natural scenery.
We’re passionate about the outdoors, dedicated to top-notch safety,
and committed to giving every guest an epic experience they’ll never forget.

Join us, and let the river do the talking.

History

Splash White Water Rafting was founded in 2010 by a small group of river enthusiasts who believed that adventure should be accessible to everyone. What started as weekend trips with friends down local rapids quickly grew into a full-scale operation driven by passion, community, and a love for the outdoors.

We've proudly introduced thousands of adventurers to the power and beauty of white water rafting — and we're just getting started. From humble beginnings to one of the region’s most trusted rafting outfitters, our journey has always been fueled by the thrill of the ride and the people we meet along the way
